## Construction patterns

A geography hook usually combines a personal anchor with forward intent:
1. **Personal anchor:** "I grew up in X" / "my mum lives in Y" / "I used to live in Z".
2. **Forward intent:** "always keen to connect with developers nearby" / "always keen to do more in the area".

Working variants:
- **Anchor only (short and clean):** "I'm SW London based and always keen to connect with developers nearby" (BX Property pattern).
- **Anchor + named-scheme question** (combines rungs 2 and 3): "Your [SCHEME] scheme looked fantastic, I used to live the other side of [PARK]" (IDM pattern).
- **Anchor + sport / school transition:** "I used to play at Trailfinders and went to prep school in Ealing" (Beale Halai pattern, W London prospects).
